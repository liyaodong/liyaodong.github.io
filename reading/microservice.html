Microservice 101

1 What's the problem it trying to solve

Trying to solve the monolith can't meeting the business requirement, i.e.: quickly release only part of the system, change tech stack of part of the system.

2 What is it?

2.1 Definition

It's a architecture style to develop a single application as a suite of small services

  • each service running on it's own process
  • use lightweight mechanisms to communicate with each other, like HTTP resources API
  • build around business capabilities
  • independently deployable
  • may have bare minimum of centralized management
